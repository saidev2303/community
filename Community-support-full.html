More support from your Community


  • National Domestic violence Hotline. Call 1-800-799-7233.
  • Indiana Poison Center. Call 1-800-222-1222.
  • Indiana 2-1-1 - a free, 24 hours service connecting you to food, shelter and house assistance, employment service, counselling resources, and much more. Call 2-1-1.
  • Gateway to Work -- a free, optional program to help you build the skills you need to get new or better jobs
  • Indiana Tobacco Quitline - a free support line to help you breaking smoking habit. Call 1-800-QUIT-NOW (1-800-784-8669), Or select Click to call on the Quitline homepage to have a specialist for you.
  • About Special Kids - Indiana's parent-to-parent organization, offering advice and resources to families of children with special needs. Call 1-800-964-4746.
  • Autism Resource Network of Indiana . Call 1-800-609-8449.
  • National Person Control Center - get free poison prevent advice and treatment. Call 1-800-222-1222.
  • Relay Indiana - call 1-800-743-3333(TTY 711) if you have hearing or speech loss and a trained person will help you talk to someone who uses a stardard phone
  • Women, Infants and Children (WIC) Program - offers healthy food to pregnant women and mothers of young children. Call 1-800-522-0874
